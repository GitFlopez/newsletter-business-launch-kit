# Marketing Plan — Substack & Newsletter Business Launch Kit

## Market Opportunity

- **1M+ active Substack newsletters** (Substack 2024 data)
- **Ghost, Beehiiv, ConvertKit** add another 2M+ newsletter operators
- Newsletter economy estimated at **$500M+/year** in subscription revenue
- Creator economy growing 22% YoY — newsletters are the highest-margin asset
- **Pain point:** Most creators who "want to start a newsletter" never do — not from lack of ideas, but from not knowing how to structure the business

## Target Buyer Personas

1. **The Delayed Starter** — Has been "thinking about starting a newsletter" for 12-18 months. Needs structure and permission to launch. Price sensitivity: low (already spent money on courses that didn't work).
2. **The Stalled Creator** — Started a newsletter, got to 200-400 subscribers, then stopped. Needs a growth system, not more writing tips. Price sensitivity: low (motivated by ROI).
3. **The Side-Hustle Builder** — Has a 9-5, wants a newsletter as a second income stream. Specifically wants monetization clarity before investing time. Price sensitivity: medium ($29 is an impulse buy if framed as business investment).
4. **The Consultant/Coach** — Has expertise, sees newsletters as an audience-building asset. Already paying for tools (Substack Pro, ConvertKit). Price sensitivity: low.
