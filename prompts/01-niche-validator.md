# Prompt 1 — Niche Validator + Positioning System (FREE)

**Use this before you write a single word. Most newsletters fail because the niche is wrong — not the writing.**

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a newsletter business strategist who has helped launch 200+ profitable newsletters. Your job is to validate my niche and build a positioning system before I write anything.

MY INPUTS:
- My expertise/topic: [WHAT YOU KNOW OR DO]
- My target audience: [WHO YOU WANT TO REACH — can be rough]
- My goal for this newsletter: [e.g., generate leads, build authority, create passive income, transition careers]
- Competitors or inspirations I've seen: [optional — any newsletters you admire or compete with]

DELIVER:

1. NICHE STATEMENT
A single sentence: "A newsletter for [specific audience] who want [specific outcome] but struggle with [specific obstacle] — written from the perspective of [your unique angle]."
Give me 3 versions: Broad (bigger audience), Specific (higher conversion), Contrarian (challenges conventional wisdom).

2. TAGLINE SYSTEM
5 tagline variations for my newsletter header/bio. Each under 12 words. Format: benefit-first, no jargon, sounds like a human wrote it.

3. AUDIENCE VALIDATION CHECKLIST
5 yes/no questions I can answer right now to confirm this niche has market fit. After each question, explain what a "no" means and how to pivot.

4. MONETIZATION FIT SCORE
Rate each monetization model 1-5 for MY specific niche:
- Paid subscriptions (Substack/Ghost)
- Sponsorships/ads
- Digital products (courses, templates)
- Consulting/coaching upsells
- Job board or community
Brief explanation for each score.

5. 3 QUICK WINS
The 3 fastest ways to get my first 100 subscribers specific to my niche and audience. No generic advice — platform-specific tactics I can start this week.
```

---

## How to Use This Output

- Pick the niche statement version that excites you most (not the safest — the most specific)
- Use the tagline as your Substack/Ghost description and Twitter/X bio immediately
- Score yourself on the validation checklist — if you get 3+ "no" answers, refine the niche before building
- Use the monetization fit scores to sequence your revenue strategy (highest scores = build first)
- Start the 3 quick wins before moving to Prompt 2

---

## Pro Tips

- **Be specific about your audience.** "Entrepreneurs" fails. "SaaS founders with 1-10 employees who are pre-Series A" works.
- **Include an obstacle.** The best niches solve a specific frustration, not just deliver information.
- **Your angle matters more than your topic.** "Leadership" is boring. "Leadership from someone who failed upward" is interesting.
