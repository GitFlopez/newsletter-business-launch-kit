## What This Skill Does

Turns any expertise into a monetized newsletter business in 4 prompts. Goes beyond writing individual issues — this builds the full business: niche validation, paid subscription pitch, 90-day content system, and a complete growth + monetization stack.

Not for: people who just want to write a newsletter. For: people who want to *build* a newsletter business.

- Consultants and coaches turning expertise into recurring income

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 (FREE) — Niche Validator + Positioning System
Defines your specific audience, content angle, and monetization fit before you write a single word. Outputs: niche statement, 3 positioning options (broad/specific/contrarian), tagline system, 5-question audience validation checklist.

### Prompt 2 — Paid Subscription Pitch + Launch Welcome Sequence
Everything a reader sees in the first 72 hours: landing page copy (above fold, value section, FAQ, CTA), 5-email welcome sequence (Day 0 confirmation → Day 1 best-of → Day 3 story → Day 7 upgrade → Day 14 win), paid tier pricing rationale.

### Prompt 3 — 90-Day Content Calendar + Issue Template System
A complete operating system: 52-week content themes, 90-day detailed calendar with issue titles + formats (essay, list, case study, interview), evergreen series structure, 3-section issue template (hook + body + CTA), re-engagement cadence.

### Prompt 4 — Growth Engine + Monetization Roadmap
Full business build: referral program copy (3-tier milestone rewards), cross-promotion pitch template for 5-10 partner newsletters, Twitter/X funnel (3 thread templates + pinned bio), sponsor kit (rate card formula, pitch deck outline, sponsor FAQ), monetization ladder (free → paid → cohort → consulting → productized service).
